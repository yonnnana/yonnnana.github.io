Product Manager

Grave Denture Manager / Sakurai Shin-ichi

Product Development Department / Joined the company in October 2023 (mid-career)

I used to work in a dental office. There, I learned that when making dentures, the patient's tooth shape, face shape, and bite can be carefully adjusted to create natural and comfortable dentures. I felt that dentures are a part of a patient's life, full of personality and memories. However, I was faced with the reality that dentures of the deceased are often thrown away or become a hindrance to the bereaved family. I believed that the soul of the deceased resided in the dentures and that they should be treated with care. At that time, I learned about grave dentures and was impressed by the wonderful idea of placing the dentures of the deceased in the grave to put the soul of the deceased to rest. I decided to become a Grave Dentures Product Manager to promote this service.
